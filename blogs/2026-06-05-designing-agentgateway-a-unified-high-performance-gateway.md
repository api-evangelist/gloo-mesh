Agentgateway is a unified, high-performance gateway built in Rust that handles AI and API traffic together, addressing operational challenges organizations face when deploying AI agents. The project, which achieved over 7 million downloads since February, was donated to the Agentic AI Infrastructure Foundation and delivers approximately 500k QPS with sub-0.2ms P99 latency in benchmark testing.
